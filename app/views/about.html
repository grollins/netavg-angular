About FlexE

FlexE is based on a simple elastic network model and uses the deformation energy as a measure of the similarity between two protein structures. FlexE incorporates the concept of thermal energy, which provides a rational way to determine when two models are “the same”. FlexE provides a unique measure of the similarity between protein structures that is complementary to existing methods. For further details, see our JCTC article, FlexE: Using Elastic Network Models to Compare Models of Protein Structure.
